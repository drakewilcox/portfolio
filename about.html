About...

Originally from Spokane, WA, I have spent the last ten years living on the rainy side of the Pacific Northwest. After graduating from Western Washington University with a degree in Communications and Audio Technology in 2015, I moved to Portland, OR. My time here is spent working as an audio engineer, producer, bartender, and most recently as a student at Epicodus in the Full-Stack Developer program. This site is meant to showcase that work and will evolve over time.

You can find me...

  1. Reading in a greasy diner with hashbrowns and coffee.
  2. Scouring the internet for songs to add to the perfect roadtrip playlist.
  3. Locked in a basement studio for days recording musical sounds and turning knobs on old tape machines.
